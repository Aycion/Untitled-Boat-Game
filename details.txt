Jeremy Tuthill

Features Implemented:
- Camera System Transition (mandatory)
- Camera System Rotation
- Camera System Scaling
- Rectangle-to-Rectangle Collision
- Circle-to-Circle Collision
- Rectangle-to-Circle Collision
- Audio System

Controls:
- W: move ship forwards
- A: rotate ship towards port (left)
- D: rotate ship towards starboard (right)
- I: zoom in with camera
- O: zoom out with camera
- F (held for ~1 second): switch between boat (rectangular) and life preserver (circular)
- SPACE: make cannon noise (cannon projectiles not supported yet)
